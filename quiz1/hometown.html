Hometown

Bondowoso, East Java

My hometown from my mother is Bondowoso to be precise in Tapen district. There the atmosphere is still beautiful, there are many stretches of mountain rice fields, and the kinship there is very much felt. The atmosphere in the morning is very cool because it is near the mountains.


Banyuwangi, East Java

My hometown from my father is Banyuwangi. The atmosphere is more urban. The sense of family is also very warm. Right across from the Baiturrahman Grand Mosque. The night atmosphere is more pleasant because in front of the house there is also a Banyuwangi town square where there are lots of toys and toys for children.
