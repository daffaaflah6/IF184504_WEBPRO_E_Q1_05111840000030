Tourist Place

Some of the tourist place in Bondowoso, East Java

The most favorite tourist location and a mainstay of Bondowoso Regency is Ijen Crater. This Ijen Bondowoso Crater Tour has a 3 km long climbing route, and can be reached in about 1.5 hours. The climb to the crater is quite difficult because it has fairly steep road access. However, you can enjoy a beauty that is second to none if you can reach the rim of the crater. Don't forget to wear a jacket, guys, because the temperature of Ijen Crater is quite cold, it can reach 2 degrees Celsius.
Mount Raung is one of the mountains that divides three regencies, namely Banyuwangi Regency, Jember Regency, and Bondowoso Regency. The climb to the top of Mount Raung is one of the most popular activities for young people. Mount Raung has an altitude of 3,332 meters above sea level, and has a caldera with a depth of 500 meters. It is necessary to be aware that Mount Raung is one of the active mountains in Java, and always emits smoke. The view from the top of this mountain is breathtaking.
Wana Wisata Wurung Crater is located in Kalianyar Village, Sempol District, Bodowoso Regency. This tourism area is managed by Perum Perhutani Bondowoso. In this place the temperature is quite cold. If the day is clear, you can take pictures with a beautiful scenic background from a height. This most beautiful tourist spot in Bondowoso is perfect for your holiday destination.

Some of the tourist place in Banyuwangi, East Java

Bangsring Underwater is located in Bangsring Village, Wongsorejo District. To be precise, it is only about 40 minutes from Ketapang Station. If you like to enjoy the beauty of the underwater ecosystem, you need to visit a tourist spot which is a conservation area managed by local fishermen. There, there are many types of cute fish, one of which is clown fish or nemo fish. He is just as cute as the cartoon version in Finding Nemo.
Gandrung dance is one of Banyuwangi's cultural identities. In fact, this dance has often been performed annually since 2011 through the Gandrung Sewu Festival. If this year the festival is not held due to a pandemic, you can still see the mad dance through a thousand gandrung statues in the Terracotta Gandrung Park. The statue is made using earthenware. If you want to take a photo with a statue, you have to be careful because the patnung is fragile and breaks easily. Even though many statues are in one place, you can also see the infatuated statue on the lip of the pool near the rice fields. This tourist spot is located at the foot of Mount Ijen. Precisely in the Jiwa Jawa Ijen Resort area, Licin District.
It can be said that the beach which is located in Sumber Agung Village, Pesanggaran Subdistrict, is a paradise for new surfers. The waves are only two meters high, making it easier for them to control the surfboard. The Alas Purwo National Park (TN) is believed to be the first forest, or the oldest forest. However, this magical air may arise from the dense trees that block the noise pollution to make the atmosphere very quiet. If you want to take a vacation while enjoying nature in peace, TN Alas Purwo is worth a visit. Following the entrance, you will be greeted by shady mahogany trees along the main road. If you want to see peacocks, deer, and Javan bulls, you can continue to the Sadengan savanna. The park, which is designated as a National Geopark, has an area of ​​44,037 hectares, and is filled with hundreds of types of flora and fauna. To be precise 700 flora, 50 types of mammals, 320 birds, 15 species of amphibians, and 48 species of reptiles. Alas Purwo National Park is located in Tegaldlimo District and Purwoharjo District.
