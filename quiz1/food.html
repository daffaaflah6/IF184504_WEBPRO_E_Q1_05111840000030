Local Food

Some of the local food in Bondowoso, East Java

One of Bondowoso specialties that you should not miss to try is Bondowoso rolled meatball culinary. What is it like? Unlike other meatballs, these meatballs are equipped with meatballs that are rolled up. The texture is very soft and melts on the tongue, it is definitely very suitable to warm the body after visiting several tourist attractions. To be able to enjoy this delicious food, you have to prepare IDR 15,000 to buy one portion of meatballs. You can find this Bondowoso meatball culinary easily in many places in Bondowoso.
Well, here it is who has become a culinary icon of this district, namely Tape Manis Bondowoso. You must buy this food for souvenirs to your hometown. Is there any difference between this tape and tapes in other areas? The answer is correct, but it has a distinctive taste that you cannot find anywhere else and the price is quite affordable, which is only around IDR 20,000. The taste is sweet and the texture is soft on the tongue is what makes this tape different from other tapes. If you want to buy this Bondowoso tape, you must first know some of the famous brands, such as Tape Handayani 82, Tape Manis 82 Tape Tjap Enak, Tape Manis 31, and others.
What is Suwar Suwir? Bondowoso typical snacks are one of the typical snacks that are made using the basic ingredients of tape. When viewed from the texture, this cake looks the same as lunkhead which has a sweet and sweet taste. It is square in shape and has a variety of attractive colors. To be able to taste the uniqueness of this snack, you can buy it at a typical Bondowoso gift shop. The price varies according to the size of the box. If the size is small, the price tag is around IDR 10,000, while for a large box the price is twice that, IDR 20,000.

Some of the local food in Banyuwangi, East Java

Rujak or Soto? Why not both, and become Rujak Soto. As the name implies, this dish is a combination of two preparations, namely soup with soup and salad without sauce. The taste is also very delicious and steady, so that it can make us get to enjoy it. If you have the opportunity to visit Banyuwangi, of course don't miss the opportunity to taste Rujak Soto.
Have you ever tasted Bagiak? This Banyuwangi specialty is made from sago tapung, and is processed using a special method. Bagiak is also often given a variety of flavors, to enrich the taste when eaten. Some of the flavors include durian, vanilla, pandan, mocca, and ginger flavor. Even though it looks hard, when it is bitten by Bagiak it is quite soft. It is not surprising that Bagiak is quite liked by all groups, from children to adults.
Bolu Kuwuk or for the people of Banyuwangi themselves, they often refer to it as soft. Just like the ingredients for sponge cake, Kelemben is also made from wheat flour mixed with sugar and eggs. It has a cute and quirky shape resembling a turtle, and is generally served with a cup of black coffee. Bolu Kuwuk is often served, on big days such as holidays and when extended families come to visit.
Setup is a typical Banyuwangi drink, where there are various fruit pieces inside. For example, with the Jackfruit Fruit Setup, what is produced is a drink that contains pieces of jackfruit in it. Very fresh fruit setup, when eaten during the day. If you visit Banyuwangi, then this drink can be an option when the sun is shining very hot.
